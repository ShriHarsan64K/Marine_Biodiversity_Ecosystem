📊 CORE PHILOSOPHY
===============================================================================

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE: 
Detect and track INDICATOR SPECIES to assess marine ecosystem health, NOT 
comprehensive fish cataloging. Focus on ecological signals over exhaustive 
species identification.

KEY INDICATORS:
- Butterflyfish (Coral health proxy - obligate corallivores)
- Grouper (Top predator - fishing pressure indicator)
- Parrotfish (Algae control - bioerosion balance)
- Surgeonfish (Herbivore function - reef balance)
- Damselfish (Habitat complexity indicator)
- Wrasse (Ecosystem services - cleaning stations)
- Triggerfish (Degradation sensitivity)
- Angelfish (Reef structure indicator)

EXPANDED INDICATORS (Phase 2):
- Sharks (Apex predator presence)
- Cleaner Wrasse (Mutualism functionality)
- Lionfish (Invasive species alert - Indo-Pacific only)

PHASE 5A: Core Biodiversity Indices (Week 11)
──────────────────────────────────────────────
IMPLEMENT:

1. SHANNON DIVERSITY INDEX (H')
   Formula: H' = -Σ(pi × ln(pi))
   Interpretation:
   - H' > 2.0: Excellent diversity
   - H' 1.5-2.0: Good diversity
   - H' 1.0-1.5: Fair diversity  
   - H' < 1.0: Poor diversity (ALERT!)

2. SIMPSON'S INDEX (D)
   Formula: D = 1 - Σ(pi²)
   Interpretation: Probability two random fish are different species

3. PIELOU'S EVENNESS (J')
   Formula: J' = H' / ln(S)
   Interpretation: How evenly distributed species are

4. SPECIES RICHNESS (S)
   Simple count of unique species detected

PHASE 5B: Weighted Health Index (Week 11)
──────────────────────────────────────────
ECOLOGICAL WEIGHTING:

Indicator Species Weights (based on ecological importance):
- Butterflyfish: w = 2.0 (coral dependency - early warning)
- Grouper: w = 1.8 (apex predator - fishing pressure)
- Parrotfish: w = 1.6 (algae control - critical function)
- Sharks: w = 2.0 (top predator - pristine indicator)
- Surgeonfish: w = 1.4 (herbivore guild)
- Cleaner Wrasse: w = 1.5 (mutualism - ecosystem services)
- Damselfish: w = 1.0 (baseline - habitat indicator)
- Wrasse: w = 1.2 (functional diversity)
- Triggerfish: w = 1.3 (sensitivity indicator)
- Angelfish: w = 1.1 (reef structure)

WEIGHTED SHANNON:
H'_w = -Σ(wi × pi × ln(pi)) / Σ(wi)

PHASE 5C: Trophic Pyramid Analysis (Week 12)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
FUNCTIONAL GROUPS:
1. Apex Predators: Sharks, large Grouper
2. Mesopredators: Triggerfish, smaller Grouper
3. Herbivores: Parrotfish, Surgeonfish
4. Planktivores: Damselfish
5. Corallivores: Butterflyfish, Angelfish
6. Cleaners: Cleaner Wrasse

TROPHIC BALANCE SCORE:
Healthy Pyramid:
- 5-10% Apex predators
- 15-20% Mesopredators  
- 35-45% Herbivores
- 25-35% Planktivores
- 5-10% Specialized (corallivores, cleaners)

Degradation Signals:
- <2% Apex → Overfishing
- >60% Herbivores → Mesopredator collapse
- <3% Corallivores → Coral loss

PHASE 5D: Composite Marine Health Index (Week 12)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────
FORMULA:
MHI = 0.30×H'_weighted + 
      0.25×Trophic_Balance + 
      0.20×Apex_Predator_Score + 
      0.15×Indicator_Presence + 
      0.10×Evenness

OUTPUT: 0-100 scale
- 80-100: Excellent (pristine reef)
- 60-79: Good (healthy reef)
- 40-59: Fair (stressed reef)
- 20-39: Poor (degraded reef)
- 0-19: Critical (ecosystem collapse)

ALERT THRESHOLDS:
🔴 CRITICAL: MHI < 30 for >5 minutes
🟡 WARNING: MHI 30-50 or declining >10 points/hour
🟢 HEALTHY: MHI > 60 and stable
